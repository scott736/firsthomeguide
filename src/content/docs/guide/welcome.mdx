Welcome to the most comprehensive, free resource for Canadian first-time home buyers. Whether you are just starting to think about homeownership or you are already saving for a down payment, this guide will walk you through every step of the process — from checking your financial readiness to picking up the keys on closing day.

## Why This Guide Exists

Buying your first home is the largest financial decision most Canadians will ever make, yet studies consistently show that **85% of first-time buyers feel unprepared** when they begin the process. The information is out there, but it is scattered across government websites, bank marketing pages, and real estate blogs — each with its own agenda.

We built FirstHomeGuide.ca to solve that problem. This is a **neutral, education-first resource** with no product upsells, no affiliate mortgage links, and no pressure to buy before you are ready. Every page is written in plain language, updated regularly, and designed to give you the confidence to make informed decisions.

## What Makes This Guide Different

- **Truly independent.** We do not sell mortgages, insurance, or real estate services. Our only goal is to educate you.
- **Province-specific.** Canada's housing rules vary significantly by province. We cover federal programs and then break down what is available in all 10 provinces.
- **Actionable.** You will find real dollar amounts, example calculations, checklists, and step-by-step timelines — not vague advice.
- **Up to date.** All content reflects the latest 2026 rules, including current [FHSA](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/individuals/topics/first-home-savings-account.html) contribution limits, the updated [HBP](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/individuals/topics/rrsps-related-plans/what-home-buyers-plan.html) withdrawal amounts, and the newest provincial incentive programs.

## The 8 Modules

This guide is organized into eight modules that follow the natural journey of buying your first home:

1. **[Are You Ready to Buy?](/guide/1-are-you-ready/1-first-time-buyer/)** — Find out if you qualify as a first-time buyer, check your credit score, understand the mortgage stress test, and honestly assess whether now is the right time for you.
2. **[Saving Smart — FHSA, RRSP HBP & More](/guide/2-saving-smart/1-fhsa/)** — Master the tax-advantaged accounts available to you, including how a couple can access up to $100,000 in combined savings.
3. **[Down Payments & Mortgage Basics](/guide/3-down-payments-mortgages/1-down-payment-rules/)** — Learn the down payment rules, understand CMHC insurance, compare fixed vs. variable rates, and get pre-approved with confidence.
4. **[Government Programs by Province](/guide/4-government-programs/1-federal-programs/)** — A complete directory of every federal and provincial program, from the Home Buyers' Tax Credit to land transfer tax rebates in your province.
5. **[Finding the Right Home](/guide/5-finding-a-home/0-your-team/)** — How to work with a buyer's agent, evaluate neighbourhoods, attend open houses, and make a competitive offer.
6. **[The Offer & Negotiation](/guide/6-making-an-offer/1-purchase-agreement/)** — Conditions, deposits, bidding strategies, and how to protect yourself in a competitive market.
7. **[Closing Day & Beyond](/guide/7-closing-the-deal/1-closing-costs-overview/)** — What to expect from your lawyer, title insurance, adjustments, and the final walkthrough.
8. **[Life as a Homeowner](/guide/8-life-after-closing/1-emergency-fund/)** — Maintenance budgets, property taxes, refinancing, and building long-term equity.

## How to Use This Guide

You can work through the modules in order — they are designed to build on each other — or jump straight to the topic you need most. Each module ends with a link to the next one so you can pick up right where you left off.

:::tip
If you are in the early planning stages, start with **[Module 1: Are You Ready to Buy?](/guide/1-are-you-ready/1-first-time-buyer/)** and work forward. If you are already saving and want to optimize your strategy, jump to **[Module 2: Saving Smart](/guide/2-saving-smart/1-fhsa/)**.
:::
