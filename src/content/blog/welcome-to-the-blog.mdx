Welcome. The core of FirstHomeGuide.ca is our [8-module guide](/guide/welcome/) — the
structured, evergreen content that walks you from "am I ready?" all the way to life after
closing. But home buying in Canada doesn't stand still. FHSA limits change. CMHC updates
premium tables. Provinces launch (and cancel) rebate programs. Rates move.

This blog is where we'll post the shorter, time-sensitive stuff:

- **Rule changes** — when federal or provincial rules shift, we'll explain what actually
  changes for buyers and what to do about it.
- **Market updates** — quarterly takes on Bank of Canada rate decisions, CMHC data, and
  what they mean for first-time buyers specifically.
- **Deep dives** — topics that don't fit neatly into the 8-module structure, like niche
  program eligibility, co-buying agreements, or pre-construction contract gotchas.
- **Answers to common questions** — the ones we get on calls often enough to write up
  properly.

If you want to follow along, [subscribe to the RSS feed](/rss.xml). Questions or ideas for
what we should cover? Send them through the [contact form](/contact).
